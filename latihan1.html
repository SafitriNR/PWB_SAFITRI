Films with Sam Spiegel Productions

The Bridge on the River Kwai (1957)

Produced by Sam Spiegel, this film was the first of the Lean blockbuster movies, and featured a young Alec Guidness, Willian Holden, and a brillian performance from Sessue Hayakawa.

Lawrence of Arabian (1962)

One of my all-time favorite movies, this epic adventure starring Peter O'Toole established Lean as a director who could truly envision film on a grand scale.
